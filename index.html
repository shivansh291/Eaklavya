Our Heritage

Founded in the sacred city of Rishikesh, Eklavya Yogshala is dedicated to preserving India's rich yoga heritage. Our gurus have trained in the traditional gurukul system under the guidance of Himalayan masters.

We blend ancient wisdom with modern teaching methods to make yoga accessible while maintaining its spiritual essence.
